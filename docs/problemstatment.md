# Problem Statement

# MacroMate

## Overview

Individuals pursuing fat loss, muscle gain, or body recomposition are often advised to track calories and macronutrients. While awareness of nutrition is high, consistently calculating the nutritional value of homemade meals remains a tedious and repetitive process.

Most users consume meals containing multiple ingredients and varying quantities. To determine nutritional values, they frequently switch between nutrition labels, search engines, spreadsheets, and tracking applications. This introduces friction and reduces adherence to nutrition tracking habits.

As a result, many users know their goals but lack an easy way to determine whether their daily food intake supports those goals.

---

## Problem

Fitness-focused individuals struggle to quickly and accurately calculate the nutritional composition of custom meals.

Existing solutions often require excessive manual input, making nutrition tracking inconvenient and difficult to sustain consistently.

---

## Target Audience

### Primary Users

* Gym-goers pursuing fat loss
* Individuals aiming to build or maintain muscle
* Users actively tracking protein intake
* People preparing meals at home

### Secondary Users

* Health-conscious individuals
* Nutrition beginners
* Individuals monitoring dietary habits

---

## Impact

When users cannot easily calculate meal nutrition:

* Protein targets are frequently missed.
* Calorie targets become difficult to manage.
* Nutrition decisions become reactive rather than intentional.
* Consistency decreases over time.

This directly impacts progress toward fitness goals.

---

## Desired Outcome

Users should be able to determine the nutritional value of a custom meal in less than one minute using a simple and intuitive workflow.
